Curriculum Vitae

Hello! I'm Soumika Saha, a PhD student in Mathematics at the University of Iowa.

My interests include applied mathematics, dynamical systems, mathematical biology, bifurcation theory.

I work on mathematical modeling, differential equations.

Prior that, I have completed B.Sc. in Mathematics from University of Calcutta and M.Sc. in Mathematics from Banaras Hindu University in 2022 under the supervision of Dr. Anupam Priyadarshi. I was born and raised in Kolkata, India. During my master's, I spend two years in Varanasi, India. After completing my master's, I was working in a govt. sponsored school as an assistant teacher in Mathematics and from Fall 2024, I am in University of Iowa.I am a native Bengali speaker.
