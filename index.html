Curriculum Vitae

Hello! I'm Soumika Saha, a PhD student in Mathematics at the University of Iowa.

My interests include applied mathematics, dynamical systems, mathematical biology, bifurcation theory.

I am interested in bifurcation analysis and non-linear dynamics of systems using ordinary differential equations, mostly the ecological models. I am also interested in the numerical methods for solving these equations.

Prior that, I have completed B.Sc. in Mathematics from University of Calcutta in 2020 and M.Sc. in Mathematics from Banaras Hindu University in 2022 under the supervision of Dr. Anupam Priyadarshi. I was born and raised in Kolkata, India.I am a native Bengali speaker but I can speak Hindi and English too.
